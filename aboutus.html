What is CarFun?

CarFun is an upcoming corporation founded by a group of dedicated automotive enthusiasts in the Philippines. Our flagship venture, "CarFun," is a dynamic enterprise that specializes in sourcing and providing high-quality, rare automotive parts to cater to the discerning needs of car aficionados and enthusiasts.

Mission

GlobalGarage Corp. and CarFun share a common mission: "To be the premier destination for automotive enthusiasts in the Philippines, providing access to rare and sought-after automotive parts, exceptional customer service, and innovative solutions through AI technology. We aim to enhance the Philippine automotive scene by empowering car owners to personalize and upgrade their vehicles with ease and style."

Vision

Our vision at GlobalGarage Corp. and CarFun is clear: "To become a globally recognized brand that not only caters to the needs of car enthusiasts but also contributes to the growth and development of the automotive aftermarket industry in the Philippines. We envision a future where CarFun is synonymous with quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction."
